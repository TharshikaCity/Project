Bootcamp 2021: Instrument Sounds

My first programming project, concluding the 2 week Programming Bootcamp at City (2021). This Processing project demonstrates a comprehensive understanding of the use of sound, images and interaction. I created a program which plays instrument sounds each time you press a number from 1 - 5 on the keyboard. When a number on the keyboard is pressed the picture of the instrument, sound of the instrument and sound frequency of the instrument is displayed on the screen. Interaction is used in order to play the instrument requested by the user and to stop the music when pressing the space bar. I used IF and FOR statements to play each instrument based on the number chosen and also to display the sound frequency of the instrumentsat the bottom of the screen.
